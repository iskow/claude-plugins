# Client Discovery Form — Master Template

_This is the source of truth for the questions. The `discovery-form` skill renders it into a client-facing file (fillable PDF / editable doc / Google-Form-ready). Edit questions here, not in the exports._

**Purpose for the client:** a quick 5–15 minute form so we understand your business and what your website/app needs to do.
**Purpose for Joel:** answers map straight into a client profile (`clients/<slug>.md`) and tell us the likely feature scope before quoting.

**Rendering rules:**
- Keep every question short-answer, pick-one, or checkbox. No essay prompts.
- Question 4 in Part 1 is conditional — only relevant if they've been operating a while.
- Preserve the two-part split. Part 1 = who they are. Part 2 = what it must do.
- In the intro of any export, tell the client: *"Skip anything that doesn't apply or you're unsure about — we'll cover the rest on a quick call."*
